Become a Sponsor and help us make Staticaly better!

You can sponsor Staticaly by:

  • Providing us your service for free (CDN, DNS, web hosting, anything else we might need)
  • Making monthly donations to help us cover development costs and other expenses
  • Donating development hours of your employees

Your sponsorship level is based on the value of your monthly donation. For example, to become a Prime sponsor, you need to sponsor a service worth $100 - 499 per month, donate the same amount of money, or corresponding number of development hours.

You can contact us anytime if you have any questions.
